porshe 911

The Porsche 911 is an iconic, rear-engine luxury sports car introduced in 1963, featuring a distinctive 2+2 seating layout and flat-six engines. Known for its rear-engine layout and high-performance, the 992 generation (2019-present) currently uses twin-turbo engines and offers models like the 394-hp Carrera, 480-hp Carrera S, and 640-hp Turbo S, with many reaching 0-100 km/h under 3.5 seconds
